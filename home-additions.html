Seamless By Design

Additions that look original to the house.

The mark of a great addition is that no one can tell where the old house ends and the new one begins. We match rooflines, siding, trim profiles, and window proportions so the new wing belongs — inside and out.

  • Matched rooflines & pitches
  • Siding & trim profiles replicated
  • Window & muntin proportions
  • Continuous flooring & ceilings
  • Aged-material sourcing
  • Period-correct detailing
