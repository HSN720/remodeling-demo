Overview

This River Oaks renovation transformed a traditional, closed-off kitchen into a bright and sophisticated open-concept gathering space designed for modern family living and entertaining. The project included the removal of a structural wall, the installation of handcrafted white oak cabinetry, a dramatic 14-foot Calacatta marble waterfall island, integrated Sub-Zero and Wolf appliances, wide-plank European oak flooring, custom brass fixtures, and expansive steel-framed garden doors that connect the interior to the outdoor living areas. Every detail was carefully selected to create a timeless kitchen that blends luxury, functionality, and enduring craftsmanship.

What We Did

We removed a major structural wall to create an open-concept layout that seamlessly connects the kitchen, dining, and living spaces. The redesign centered around a dramatic 14-foot Calacatta marble waterfall island, creating a natural gathering place for family and guests. Custom white oak cabinetry was handcrafted throughout the space, paired with integrated Sub-Zero and Wolf appliances, wide-plank European oak flooring, bespoke brass fixtures, and steel-framed garden doors that flood the interior with natural light.

The Approach

The project began by rethinking circulation and daylight throughout the home. By removing the existing load-bearing wall, we established a continuous visual connection between the kitchen and surrounding living areas. Material selections focused on timeless warmth and longevity: white oak, natural marble, unlacquered brass, and handcrafted finishes. Every design, engineering, millwork, lighting, and installation decision was managed under one roof to ensure a cohesive final result.

"They completely transformed how our family lives in this home. The kitchen feels brighter, larger, and more welcoming than we ever imagined, while still feeling timeless and authentic to River Oaks."

The Result

The finished space delivers everything the homeowners envisioned: an elegant chef's kitchen designed for entertaining, daily family life, and long-term value. Natural light now flows through the heart of the home, indoor and outdoor living areas connect seamlessly, and every detail reflects exceptional craftsmanship and enduring luxury.
